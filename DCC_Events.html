Sports & Recreation

Athletics, fitness, and recreation are a vibrant part of living and learning at DCC Alumni. Our extensive athletic facilities — open to all students — include excellent playing fields, an 8,000-square-foot fitness center, pool, courts, and an ice rink. Beyond DCC Alumni, you can jog the Norwottuck Trail that borders campus, kayak on the Connecticut River, or mountain-bike the Robert Frost Trail.
