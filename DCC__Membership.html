Alumni Member of DCC

If you passed through the four walls of DCC, you are automatically a member of the DC College Alumni Association. We exist to keep every one of us connected to one another and to DCC for the rest of time.

Nearly 20 years later, the DCC Alumni Association has grown tremendously but the core purpose remains the same. In 2018, the association updated its mission statement to read: “For our alumni, family, and friends; we strengthen bonds to each other and to DCC; help them to thrive in faith, service, learning, and work; and inspire them to act as forces for good in their communities and the world.” It’s a charge we take seriously, which is why we spent much of 2020 evaluating ourselves and our target.

Your DCC Alumni membership means::

  • Access to powerful career tools that can help you advance
  • Invitations to networking events in your area, fun alumni get-togethers, and educational programs by superstar faculty and other members of the BU community
  • Support for scholarships, clubs, student programs, Alumni Directory & Online Communities
  • Exclusive travel and entertainment experiences
  • Life Member fleece jacket and other gear
